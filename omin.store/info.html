Shipping


Delivery is made with the courier company ECONT EXPRESS, usually within 1 working day. If you have preferences for another courier company, please contact us to clarify the details.

All orders placed after 4:00pm on a weekday are processed and shipped the next business day and are received the following business day (including Saturdays if the customer wishes).

All orders placed over the weekend are processed and shipped on Monday and received on Tuesday or Wednesday.

All orders placed after hours, on weekends or holidays are processed on the first or second business day after the day the order is received.

Each customer of omin reserves the right to request different delivery conditions in case of need, by contacting us in advance to specify the details.

Shipping only in Bulgaria!







Privacy Policy


When you shop with us or create an account, we collect some personal information from you like your name, email address, billing and shipping addresses, phone number, and payment information. We also collect technical information about your device and how you use our website through the use of cookies and other tracking technologies. And, we keep track of your browsing and buying behavior on our site to better understand what you like and to make your shopping experience even better.

We use your information to process and fulfill your orders, provide customer support, and keep you updated on the latest fashion and promotions (if you choose to subscribe to our newsletter). We may also use your information to personalize your shopping experience and improve our website and product offerings.

We work with trusted third-party service providers to help us run our business. This may include processing orders, delivering packages, and analyzing data. We only share the information necessary for them to perform their specific services and they are not allowed to use your information for any other purposes.

You're in control of your information! If you don't want to receive promotional emails from us, simply click the "unsubscribe" link in any of our emails. And, you can access and update your account information and preferences at any time by logging into your account.



Return Policy


At Omin, customer satisfaction is our top priority. That's why we offer a flexible and hassle-free return policy for all our customers. If for any reason you're not completely satisfied with your purchase, you can initiate a return within 30 days of receipt. Simply send us a massage at our contact us form and let us know the reason for your return.

Please note that in order to be eligible for a return, the items must be unworn, unwashed, and in their original packaging. If the item(s) meet these requirements, we will process your return and issue a refund back to your original payment method.

We want your shopping experience with Omin to be enjoyable and stress-free, and if you have any questions or concerns about our return policy, please don't hesitate to reach out to us.
